Welcome to Urban Space

Our project offers an AI-powered platform that analyzes urban environmental data to classify 80 localities in Bangalore into Red, Yellow, and Green Zones based on green space, population density, air quality, and infrastructure using K-Means clustering. It features an interactive map with location-based insights and chatbot support. Citizens can report environmental issues through a feedback system that links to responsible authorities, helping validate AI findings. The platform also maps sunlight radiation to recommend suitable plants for each area and suggests locations for new green spaces based on pollution and park density, empowering smarter, greener urban development.
